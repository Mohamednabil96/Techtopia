Warranty

All products sold on our website come with a manufacturer's warranty, which covers defects in materials and workmanship. The length of the warranty varies by product and is specified on the product page. If you need to make a warranty claim, please contact our customer service team.

Returns

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it to us within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange. To be eligible for a return, the product must be in its original packaging and in new, unused condition. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Exceptions

  • Software downloads.
  • Gift cards.
  • Opened consumable items (e.g. ink cartridges, batteries).

Shipping

If you are returning an item, you are responsible for the cost of shipping it back to us. We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance. We are not responsible for any items lost or damaged during shipping.

Refunds

We will process your refund within 5 business days of receiving your returned item. Refunds will be credited to the original payment method. If you paid with a gift card, your refund will be issued as a new gift card.
